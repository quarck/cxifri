Purpose of Cxifi app is encryption and decryption of short text messages. 

Application allows users to store encryption key in the app. This is the only information ever stored by the app. 
Password-based keys are stored in form of SHA256 based hash, original password is never kept. 

Absolutely no information is ever transmitted by the app itself to any clouds / 3rd party / any internet servers.

Camera permission is used by the app to scan QR code with the key when sharing they encryption key with another participant. 
No camera images are ever saved or transmitted to any destination.
